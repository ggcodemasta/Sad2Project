Go biking in Vancouver's beautiful Stanley Park.

Welcome to Vancouver! Here's how to go from sitting behind your computer to biking in Stanley Park.

  1. Browse our online selection including tandems and children's bikes.
  2. Choose a bike.
  3. Book it and pay your deposit online.
  4. Meet us at our store in Stanley Park to pick up your bike. Give staff your order number so they can assist you.
  5. Enjoy your ride!

P.S don't forget to return the bike to us to get your deposit back!
