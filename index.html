Go biking in Vancouver's beautiful Stanley Park.

Welcome to Vancouver! Here's how to go from sitting behind your computer to biking in Stanley park in 8 easy steps

  1. Browse our complete selection of bikes including tandems and children's bikes.
  2. Choose the bike that you want to ride
  3. Check its availability
  4. Book the bike
  5. Pay deposit online
  6. Haul ass to the entrance of the beautiful Stanley park where we are conveniently located
  7. Give staff your order number so they can assist you with payment
  8. *Optional* : Ask for a bike buddy; check to see if one of our staff members are up for a ride as well
  9. Enjoy your ride!

P.S Just don't forget to return the bike to us to get your deposit back!
